================================================================
  MIKE'S HVAC — AI-AGENT APP DEMO
  Operating Guide for HVAC-mobile-steps
================================================================

This is a step-by-step walkthrough of Mike's HVAC AI-agent
app. The app demonstrates a complete working day: overnight
AI-to-AI lead handling, field work, permit filing, and
invoicing — all guided by an AI agent.

A progress bar at the top of every screen shows your current
step (e.g. "3/11") and tells you exactly what to do next.
The next recommended button glows with an orange outline.
Follow all 11 steps in order for the full experience.

To restart the demo at any time, tap the small "↺" reset
button in the top-right of the progress bar.

STEP 1 — Voice AI Demo  (index.html)
--------------------------------------
Page: Home (🏠)

The step bar at the top reads "0/11" and instructs:
  "Tap 🎙️ 'Talk to Agent' on the home screen to try the
   voice AI"

Both the "🎙️ Talk to Agent" button at the bottom of the
home feed AND the floating orange bar above the navigation
glow with an orange outline.

  ▶ NEXT: Tap either glowing "🎙️ Talk to Agent" button.

The chat overlay expands from the button with a smooth
animation. A waveform appears and the agent starts
"listening". After ~2 seconds you'll see the transcript:
  "What's the status of today's jobs?"

The agent replies with:
  "You have 2 jobs today: Sarah Chen at 7 AM (furnace
   replacement — complete ✓) and Marco Garcia at 11 AM
   (AC + furnace install — in progress 🔧). Parts from
   Ferguson arrive at 8 AM."

You can also type any message in the text bar and the agent
will respond with relevant information about jobs, leads,
parts orders, or permit status.

Tap "‹" (back) to close the chat overlay. The step bar
advances to 1/11.

------------------------------------------------------

---------------------------------------------------

Navigate to Field Mode via the bottom nav or via "Go to
Field →" from Sarah's lead detail.

The screen shows the app is LIVE and listening. The
transcript preview already shows what Mike dictated:
