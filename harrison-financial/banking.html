Got Questions?

Banking FAQs

Is Harrison Financial a real bank? +
Harrison Financial is a financial technology company, not a chartered bank. Banking services are provided by our partner bank, Meridian Bank, N.A., Member FDIC. Your deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000.
Are there any fees I should know about? +
We charge zero monthly fees, zero overdraft fees, and zero minimum balance fees. Some optional services (same-day ACH, outgoing wire transfers, expedited card delivery) carry small fees, which are always disclosed before you confirm.
How quickly can I open an account? +
Most accounts are opened in under 5 minutes. You'll need a government-issued photo ID, your Social Security Number, and a funding source. We'll verify your identity in real time using our KYC process.
How many ATMs can I use for free? +
Harrison Financial members have access to 55,000+ in-network ATMs nationwide at no charge. Out-of-network ATM fees are $2.50 per transaction. We refund up to $10/month in out-of-network ATM fees for members with direct deposit.
Can I deposit cash? +
Yes. Cash deposits are available at over 90,000 retail locations including CVS, Walgreens, Dollar General, and Walmart through our Green Dot® and MoneyPass® partnerships. Fees set by the retail partner may apply (typically $4.95).
