Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I dispute a transaction? +
Open the Harrison app, navigate to the transaction in question, and tap "Dispute this charge." You'll be prompted to select a reason and provide details. Most disputes are resolved within 5–7 business days, and provisional credit is typically issued within 24 hours while we investigate.
My card was lost or stolen. What do I do? +
Open the Harrison app and tap Cards → Freeze Card immediately. This stops all new transactions. Then go to Cards → Report Lost/Stolen to cancel the card and order a replacement. A new card will arrive within 5–7 business days, with expedited delivery available. Your virtual card remains active during this time.
I can't log in to my account. What should I do? +
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the prompts to reset your credentials via email or SMS. If you're locked out due to failed attempts, your account will unlock after 30 minutes or you can contact our support team directly. Never share your password or OTP codes with anyone, including Harrison Financial employees.
How long do transfers take? +
P2P transfers between Harrison members are instant. ACH transfers to external banks take 1–3 business days (standard) or same business day (expedited, $2.99 fee). Domestic wires are processed same business day if submitted before 3 PM ET. Zelle® transfers typically complete within minutes.
How do I close my account? +
We're sorry to see you go! To close your account, contact our support team by phone or chat. We'll verify your identity, ensure your balance is transferred out, and close the account within 3–5 business days. Make sure to update any automatic payments or direct deposits beforehand.
