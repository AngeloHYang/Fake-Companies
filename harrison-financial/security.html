Account Security

Multiple Ways to Verify It's You

Harrison Financial supports a range of authentication methods. Configure them in the app and require stronger authentication for high-value actions.

👤

Face ID / Touch ID

Biometric authentication using your device's secure hardware. The fastest and most secure option for daily logins.

🔐

Authenticator App (TOTP)

Use Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP-compatible app for time-based one-time codes. Highly resistant to phishing.

📲

SMS / Email OTP

One-time codes sent via text or email for users without an authenticator app. Required for new device logins.

🔑

Hardware Security Keys

FIDO2/WebAuthn hardware keys (YubiKey, etc.) for the highest level of phishing-resistant authentication.

📞

Voice Call Verification

Automated phone call verification available for account recovery and high-value wire transfers.

🏦

Knowledge-Based Auth

Security questions and personal financial data challenges for account recovery as a last resort option.

Regulatory Compliance

KYC & AML: Non-Negotiable Standards

Financial crime compliance isn't optional—it's foundational. Harrison Financial maintains rigorous KYC and AML programs that exceed regulatory requirements.

🆔 Know Your Customer (KYC)

Every account applicant undergoes document verification (government-issued ID), liveness check, and identity database screening before an account is opened. We use Jumio and Alloy for real-time verification.

BSA § 326 · CIP Rule · OFAC Screening

🔍 Transaction Monitoring (AML)

Our AML engine monitors transaction patterns against typological red flags: structuring, smurfing, round-dollar transactions, and cross-border flows. Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) are filed with FinCEN as required.

BSA / FinCEN · SAR Filing · FinCEN 314(a)

📋 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)

Higher-risk customers—including PEPs (politically exposed persons), high-volume business accounts, and users in high-risk geographies—receive enhanced monitoring and periodic re-verification.

FATF Recommendations · PEP Screening

⚖️ Regulatory Reporting

Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) are automatically filed for cash transactions over $10,000. Our compliance team works directly with federal regulators and responds to 314(a) requests within the required timeframe.

CTR Filing · 314(a) / 314(b) · FinCEN
