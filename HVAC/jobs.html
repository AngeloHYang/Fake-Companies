The Full Journey

Every Stage — Agent-Augmented

From the moment a homeowner's AC breaks to the warranty card filed three months later, the agent removes admin from every stage of the job.

Stage 1
Lead Generation
Customer calls, texts, or their home agent reaches out. Your agent answers 24/7, qualifies the lead, and books the appointment.
Agent replaces: voicemail + callback
Stage 2
Qualification & Triage
Equipment type, photos, urgency scoring, service area check. Handled in the initial agent conversation — no owner involved.
Agent replaces: back-and-forth calls
Stage 3
Quoting & Estimating
Live pricing from Ferguson, Grainger, and Johnstone. Agent builds the quote and sends to customer before the tech even arrives.
Agent replaces: manual supplier calls
Stage 4
Scheduling & Dispatch
Route optimization, tech skill matching, ETA texts to customer. Reschedules automatically when a job runs long.
Agent replaces: whiteboard dispatch
Stage 5
Pre-Job Prep
Morning brief, parts orders placed the night before, customer history pulled, permit paperwork started.
Agent replaces: Sunday-night prep
Stage 6
On-Site Execution
Tech talks to field agent for mid-job supplier orders, customer updates, and schedule shifts. Human stays human.
Agent augments: field communication
Stage 7
Invoicing & Payment
Auto-invoice post-job. Payment reminder agent. Financing application handled in-chat. Chase unpaid invoices automatically.
Agent replaces: Sunday-night invoicing
Stage 8
Follow-Up & Retention
Maintenance plan offers, warranty registrations, filter reminders, review requests, and referral asks — all timed and automated.
Agent replaces: CRM follow-up
Stage 9
Back-Office & Compliance
EPA refrigerant tracking, license renewal reminders, insurance docs, payroll prep, and equipment depreciation logs.
Agent replaces: Sunday admin nights
