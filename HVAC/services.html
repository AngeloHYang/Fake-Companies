FAQ

Common Questions

If you don't see your question here, ask our agent — available 24/7.

How quickly can you get here for a no-AC call? ›
For emergency calls (no AC in heat over 90°F, no heat below 40°F, households with children or elderly), we aim to dispatch within 1–2 hours. Our agent will give you an honest ETA based on current technician availability when you contact us.
Do I need to be home for a service call? ›
Yes, someone 18+ should be present for diagnostics and repairs. Our agent will confirm the appointment time and send a text when the technician is 30 minutes away.
How does your AI agent quoting work? ›
When you describe your issue, our agent queries live pricing from our supplier accounts (Ferguson, Grainger, Johnstone Supply) to provide an accurate estimate before the tech arrives. Final quote is confirmed on-site after diagnosis.
Do you pull permits for new installations? ›
Yes — for all new installations and equipment replacements. Our agent files the permit application with the city building department and coordinates the inspection. This is included in all installation quotes.
What financing options are available? ›
We offer consumer financing through GreenSky (0% APR for 12 months on approved credit) for jobs over $2,500. Our agent can walk you through the application in the same chat thread — no separate website needed.
What brands do you service and install? ›
We service all major residential brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, Amana, Rheem, Ruud, York, Bryant, and Daikin. For commercial, we also service Mitsubishi and LG VRF systems. We install Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, and Daikin equipment — your agent can explain the trade-offs for your specific situation.
