Deep Dives

Common scenarios, solved

⚡

High-throughput real-time transaction authorization

Your payment flow processes thousands of transactions per second and your authorization SLA is under 200ms total. SecureStar's fraud scoring must fit inside that window without compromise.

How SecureStar handles it

  • Dedicated inference cluster with p99 <80ms contractual guarantee
  • Kafka-native consumer eliminates HTTP round-trip overhead
  • Pre-computed feature caches reduce per-request compute
  • Circuit breaker: safe fallback score on timeout (configurable)

Outcome

  • Total latency budget preserved even at peak load
  • Zero changes to authorization flow required
  • Automatic scale-out during traffic spikes
  • SLA backed by financial penalty clause in enterprise contracts
🔍

Regulatory examination — AML program audit

Your regulator asks why a set of transactions were not flagged. You need to reproduce every decision, show what signals were considered, and demonstrate the model is not a black box.

How SecureStar handles it

  • Every decision stored with full feature attribution vector
  • Immutable log queryable by transaction ID, date range, or rule hit
  • One-click SAR export in FinCEN, FATF, and EBA formats
  • Human-readable explanation attached to every flagged event

Outcome

  • Compliance team can answer examiner questions in minutes, not days
  • Model behavior is defensible and interpretable
  • Audit trail covers decisions back to your go-live date
  • Reduces time-to-respond to regulatory inquiries by ~70%
🎯

Model performance — reducing false positives

Your off-the-shelf fraud model is flagging too many legitimate transactions, creating friction for good customers and overloading your review queue.

How SecureStar handles it

  • Fine-tune on your own labeled transaction history via API
  • Adjust approve/flag/decline thresholds independently
  • Shadow mode: test new thresholds on live traffic before switching
  • Real-time precision/recall dashboard tracks impact of changes

Outcome

  • Typical 20–35% reduction in false positives after first tuning cycle
  • No waiting on vendor engineering team for threshold changes
  • Model learns from your specific customer behavior patterns
  • Review queue shrinks, analyst capacity freed for high-risk cases
